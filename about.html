My Journey

Beyond the chains of Patriarchy

Growing up with 3 brothers in a patriarchal culture - masculine energy is all I knew. My world view and belief system has been shaped to fit this masculine structure. I wanted to belong and to be accepted by my close environment and the external world. So naturally, I subconsciously adopted many masculine traits and almost neglected completely my natural feminine traits. I tried to have my voice heard, to succeed in my job, to get promoted, better salary, more people in my team and show the world that I belong!

Unfortunately, stories like mine are still all too common among women.

We have internalized a false message about how to live and present ourselves that goes against our nature.

Over the years, and with my own personal healing journey, I realized that there is no need to hold an image which is not completely myself. I realized that I was terrified to been considered "a girl". I was afraid to be considered weak, not professional, emotional, "too" feminine. I lost the connection to my feminine and many years later I came back to myself, to my feminine power, my personal power, my compassion, my wide view, my intuition and allowance. My masculine traits started to balance in with my feminine ones and then it all started to feel aligned again.

After 15 years in management positions in the consultancy and corporate world I've embarked a new journey. A journey with a mission to embrace the feminine in everything we do and balance the current power structures in our society. Same achievements, less resistance.

Let's all embrace both feminine and masculine which are present within us to have the best human experience we can possibly have and lead from there!

Join me on a personal journey to embrace your full self!
